Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

AeroAtmos is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your data:

  • No data collection. AeroAtmos does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • No analytics. We do not use any analytics or tracking frameworks.
  • No accounts. The app does not require registration or sign-in.
  • Barometric sensor. The app reads barometric pressure from your device's sensor for live atmosphere data. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted.
  • Location. If you grant permission, the app uses GPS altitude for atmosphere calculations. Location data is processed on-device only and is never stored or shared.

All atmospheric calculations are performed locally on your device. No internet connection is required to use the app.

About

AeroAtmos computes atmospheric properties from sea level to 51 km using the ICAO Standard Atmosphere model (Doc 7488/3). Built for aerospace engineering students, pilots, and aviation enthusiasts.

  • Temperature, pressure, density, speed of sound & viscosity
  • Interactive atmosphere profile charts
  • Density altitude calculator
  • Complete ICAO reference table & equations
  • Live barometer with ISA comparison
